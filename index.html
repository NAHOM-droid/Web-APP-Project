Real-time Road Signs Detection and Recognition for Enhanced Road Safety

Road sign detection and recognition play a critical role in improving driver safety and awareness in the modern traffic era. This paper describes the development and evaluation of a Road Sign Detection and Recognition System (RSDRS). Our system leverages computer vision techniques and mobile application technology to provide drivers with real-time visual and auditory feedback based on detected traffic signs.
